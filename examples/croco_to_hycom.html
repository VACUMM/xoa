Compare CROCO and HYCOM outputs¶

This notebooks uses xoa to facilitate the comparison between CROCO and HYCOM models outputs.
This involves : * the automatic discovery of names in dataset thanks the `xoa.cf <https://vacumm.github.io/xoa/api/xoa.cf.html>`__ module, * the conversion from sigma to depths coordinates thanks the xoa.sigma module, * the conversion from layer thickness to depths coordinates thanks to the xoa.grid module, * the horizontal and vertical regridding to colocate outputs thanks to the xoa.regrid module.
