xoa.sigma.atmosphere_sigma_to_pressures¶

xoa.sigma.atmosphere_sigma_to_pressures(sig, ps, ptop, cache=None)[source]¶

Convert from sigma [0, 1] to altitude in an atmopsheric model

Source: Ocean sigma coordinate

Formula:

\[p = p_{top} + \sigma * (p_{surf}-p_{top})\]

Sigma standard name: atmosphere_sigma_coordinate

Formula terms: sigma: var1 ps: var2 ptop: var3

Parameters

  • sig (xarray.DataArray) – Sigma coordinates range from 0 to 1 (\(\sigma\) | sigma)

  • ps (xarray.DataArray) – Surface air pressure (\(p_{surf}\) | ps)

  • ptop (xarray.DataArray) – Air pressure at top of model (\(p_{top}\) | ptop)

Returns

Air pressure in Pa (\(p\))

Return type

xarray.DataArray
